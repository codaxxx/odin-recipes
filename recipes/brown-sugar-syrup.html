Brown Sugar Syrup

Description

Simple syrup is made with two ingredients: sugar and water. But you can get really creative with simple syrups be infusing them with herbs or fruit to add even more flavor.

In this particular recipe, weâ€™re using dark brown sugar and water and I like to add a teaspoon of vanilla to really smooth out and heighten the flavor.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Add water and sugar to saucepan
  2. Allow the mixture to boil
  3. Stir occasionally until the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Once sugar is dissolved, add vanilla and enjoy!
